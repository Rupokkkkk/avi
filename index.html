Md Abrar Jahin

CS PhD Student & Graduate Fellow, USC Viterbi | AI Researcher, Center on Knowledge Graphs, USC ISI

This is Md Abrar Jahin, CSCA; however, you can call me Abrar.

I am a CS PhD student at the Thomas Lord Department of Computer Science, Viterbi School of Engineering, University of Southern California (USC). I am affiliated with the Center on Knowledge Graphs at the Information Sciences Institute (ISI), located in Silicon Beach. I am co-advised by Prof. Craig Knoblock and Prof. Jay Pujara. My research at ISI is supported by the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA). I also received the USC Graduate Fellowship for my first year.

Before USC, I graduated in Industrial & Production Engineering (2024) from Khulna University of Engineering and Technology (KUET).

My research focuses on efficient deep learning, quantum machine learning, geometric deep learning, and trustworthy AI. I'm also interested in high-energy physics, healthcare, supply chain optimization, and more.
